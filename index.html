FAQ

Questions we get every week.

Don't see yours? WhatsApp Arvin directly and get a straight answer.

What's included in the RM 1,000/month?
Everything. Unlimited guards (30 or 3,000 — same price). Unlimited user accounts. All 20+ modules. All future feature releases. Priority WhatsApp support. Quarterly reviews. Data export anytime. No per-seat fees, no per-guard fees, no feature tiers. One price, one product, no surprises.
What's the RM 2,500 setup fee for?
Real time we invest: tenant provisioning, data migration from your current Excel/WhatsApp/paper systems, two 1-hour training sessions for your team, and 30 days of priority WhatsApp support through go-live. Non-refundable because we've spent real hours on it — we'd rather be honest about that than bury it in a higher monthly rate.
How does the 30-day money-back pilot work?
You pay RM 2,500 setup + RM 1,000 for month 1. If within 30 days you decide Noryx isn't a fit, we refund the RM 1,000 monthly in full, export your complete database in CSV/JSON, and you keep the setup fee (which covered the migration work). No pressure, no guilt, no retention calls.
What happens when the 20 Founding Member slots fill?
New customers still pay the same RM 1,000/month — but without the lifetime price lock. So when we raise prices later, those later customers will pay the new rate. Founding Members stay at RM 1,000/month forever, regardless. The 20 slots are how we reward the first 20 companies who take the leap.
Is our data secure? What about PDPA?
Every piece of guard PII (IC, passport, bank account, salary) is AES-256 encrypted at rest with per-tenant salts. Data hosted in Singapore on Supabase Pro — not the US, so no CLOUD Act exposure. Noryx Sdn Bhd is registered as your PDPA data processor, with a formal DPA signed as part of onboarding.
What if Noryx goes down?
Hosted on Supabase Pro with 99.9% uptime SLA — same infrastructure used by fintech companies and payment processors. If there's an outage, you have the founder's direct WhatsApp, not a ticket queue. Expected maintenance windows are communicated 72 hours in advance.
What if you close down or get acquired?
Your data is always yours — full export available anytime in standard formats. Founding Member pricing and data ownership terms are written into your contract. Practically: Noryx has real revenue from day one, with a paying production customer and a founder who has 30+ years of family history in this industry. If the unthinkable happens, you're never locked in.
How long does onboarding take?
14 days from payment to go-live. Day 1: kickoff call. Day 3–7: we migrate your data. Day 7–14: two training sessions. Day 14: you're live. Day 14–30: daily WhatsApp check-ins, soft launch with founder on standby.
Can you customize Noryx for our specific workflow?
Specific one-off customizations: no, to keep the product stable for every customer. But Founding Members vote on the roadmap — your feature requests have real weight. 90% of what Malaysian security companies need, Noryx already has. The remaining 10% we build in order of demand.
Our team isn't very tech-savvy. Will they use it?
Built with that in mind. If your team can use WhatsApp, they can use Noryx — large buttons, simple flows, Malay interface option, mobile-responsive. Two training sessions during onboarding, and we stay on WhatsApp with your team for 30 days. Most operations staff are productive within a week.
Is this suitable for a 500+ guard operation?
Yes. The current production tenant runs several hundred guards across 50+ sites on Noryx today, and the multi-tenant architecture comfortably handles thousands per tenant. Larger operations actually get more value because admin time savings scale with headcount.
